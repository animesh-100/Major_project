VITEEE

What is VITEEE ?

Vellore Institute of Technology Engineering Entrance Examination Vellore Institute of Technology Engineering Entrance Examination (VITEEE) is the common entrance test for admission into VIT Vellore, VIT Chennai, VIT Bhopal and VIT Andhra Pradesh. The VITEEE 2020 now, consists of a newly included Aptitude Section. Prior to the addition, the entrance exam comprised of MPCEA (Maths / Physics / Chemistry / English / Aptitude) for B.Tech Courses and BPCEA (Biology / Physics / Chemistry / English / Aptitude) for Bio Stream programmes.
